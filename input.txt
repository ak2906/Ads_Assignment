In a new essay, New
York Times Magazine
deputy editor
Jessica Lustig describes
the impact her husband’s
diagnosis has
had on her family’s life.
In this article, I will compare the reference 
genome of COVID-19 with the reference 
genomes of two different coronaviruses discovered
previously; 
one human coronavirus and one bat coronavirus.
Lustig outlines the logistics of caring for T
while quarantining herself, she describes
sending friends to scour nearby stores
for Tylenol, keeping family and friends
updated in group chats, and disinfecting
every surface in her home.
But throughout, Lustig also
describes the virus’s emotional toll.
